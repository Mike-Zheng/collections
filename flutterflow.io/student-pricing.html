Education Pricing

Current college/university students and educators are eligible to receive a free one year trial of FlutterFlow Premium

To qualify for student pricing you must be:

  • At least 18 years of age
  • Currently enrolled full-time at an accredited public or private university or college

To qualify for educator pricing you must be:

  • Currently employed at an accredited public or private university or college

Proof of Eligibility

  • At this time, we require a school-issued .edu or .ac email address to sign up
  • A verification email will be sent to your school-issued email address. Your account will not be created until this step is complete.
